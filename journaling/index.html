~BULLET JOURNALING~

PURPOSE

The purpose of this website is to excite people about journaling, help them de-stress, and better organize their everyday lives!

WHAT IS BULLET JOURNALING?

Bullet journaling is a customizable way to organize your life and make it more managable. Some bullet journals are very focused on planning and efficiency, while others are artsy and fun. Any which way, a bullet journal is meant to be a planner that you can do whatever you want with!

SUPPLIES
There are numerous amounts of supplies you can use for journalling, from a few pens to a whole art kit. Here I'll give you some commonly used to get you going!

Fineliners

Fineliners are markers that come in many different colors that have a very fine tip. Normally used for outlining and writing, they are a staple in most bullet journals.

Pens

You probably have countless pens around your house but most journals have thick paper that will work much better with high quality gel pens.

Markers

Markers are used to color, decorate, and embelish the spreads in your bullet journal. While they aren't necesseary to journalling, they can be a great and exciting edition to yours!
THINGS TO INCLUDE
You can include some many things in a bullet journal, but it is common to include calenders, planners, and other organizational spreads. However, there are plenty of things that can give your journal more personality!

Planners
Planners are what bullet journals are all about! They help you organize your life, remember important dates, and everything in between!

Mood Trackers

Mood trackers can help you recongize and pick up on behaviors you feel throughtout the month so yu can reflect on yourself and your emotions.

Habit Trackers

Habit trackers, similar to mood trackers, help you understand and keep track of things you might forget about like water or food intake. Tracking this can help you understand your behaviors and see what you can improve on!
